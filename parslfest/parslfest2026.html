APeX Summit 2026 — Academy, Parsl, funcX/Globus Compute (Hybrid) — Dates Coming Soon

The APeX Summit (Academy, Parsl, funcX/Globus Compute) is the eighth annual community meeting, bringing together researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts worldwide to discuss experiences using and developing Academy, Parsl, and Globus Compute (formerly known as funcX) – a federated function-as-a-service platform related to Parsl.
