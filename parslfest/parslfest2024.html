Registration and Call for Lightning Talks

Registration is free and open! We encourage you to share your work and experience using Parsl and/or Globus Compute with the community in a lightning talk. Presentation recordings will be posted to our site after the event and is an excellent opportunity to raise awareness of your work/project. Friday Afternoon Session: üçÑ Level Up for Power Users

By popular demand, our Friday afternoon session will be a skill enhancement opportunity called üçÑ Level Up ‚Äì an advanced session led by Ben Clifford that dives deeper into Parsl's codebase. This session, aimed at Parsl power users, will consist of six 25-minute sessions that focus on the following topics:

  • Running a task: from app definition to an htex worker and back again
  • Getting objects to workers: pickle, with an emphasis on common Parsl problems such as (un)installed packages, functions and exceptions
  • Blocks: batch systems, providers, launchers, scaling
  • Elaborating tasks: dependencies, retries, checkpointing, file staging, join_apps
  • Understanding the monitoring database
  • Plugging your own code into Parsl. How and why?
