Parsl & funcX Fest 2022 - The Parsl/funcX Community Meeting

We are hosting the fourth Parsl & funcXFest Community Meeting as a hybrid meeting on September 13-14, 2022.

The meeting brings together researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts from around the world to discuss experiences using and developing Parsl and funcX.

We have limited travel support available to attend the workshop. Please contact Kyle Chard (chard@uchicago.edu) for information.
