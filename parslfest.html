ParslFest - The Parsl Community Meeting

The annual ParslFest community meeting brings together researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts from around the country to discuss experiences using Parsl and to learn more about Parsl.

Globus Compute + ParslFest 2025

The seventh annual ParslFest community meeting will be held as a hybrid meeting on August 28–29, 2025. The meeting includes presentations from researchers, developers, cyberinfrastructure experts, and the Parsl team on the afternoon of the 26th and the morning of the 27th. The in-person component will be held at the John Crear Library (Room 390) on the University of Chicago campus.

ParslFest 2024

The sixth annual ParslFest community meeting was held as a hybrid meeting on September 26–27, 2024. The meeting included presentations from researchers, developers, cyberinfrastructure experts, and the Parsl team on the afternoon of the 26th and the morning of the 27th. The in-person component will be held at the Illini Center in Chicago, IL.

ParslFest 2023

The fifth ParslFest community meeting was held as a free hybrid meeting on October 19-20, 2023 at the University of Chicago. The meeting included presentations from researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts, as well as the Parsl team on the afternoon of the 19th and morning of the 20th. We held an optional hackathon on the afternoon of the 20th.

Parsl & funcX Fest 2022

The fourth ParslFest community meeting was held as a free hybrid meeting on September 13-14. The meeting included presentations from researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts as well as the Parsl team.

Parsl & funcX Fest 2021

The third ParslFest community meeting was held as a free virtual meeting over two sessions (Oct 27 morning and Oct 28 afternoon). The meeting included presentations from researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts as well as the Parsl team.

ParslFest 2020

The second ParslFest community meeting was held as a free virtual meeting over two sessions (Oct 6 morning and Oct 7 afternoon). The meeting included presentations from researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts as well as the Parsl team.

ParslFest 2019

The first ParslFest community meeting brought together 50 researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts for a two-day meeting that included presentations and discussions on the first day and tutorials, advanced topics, and helpdesk sessions on the second day.

The ParslFest 2019 page includes the agenda and all presentations from ParslFest 2019.
