ParslFest - The First Parsl Community Meeting

Join us for the first Parsl Community Meeting to be held October 3-4, 2019 at the University of Chicago.

The meeting will bring together researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts from around the country to discuss experiences using Parsl and contribute to developing the Parsl roadmap.

The two day meeting will include presentations and discussions on the first day and tutorials, advanced topics, and helpdesk sessions on the second day.
